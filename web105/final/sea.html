Sea World

At Sea world you wont be able to believe your eyes on how many different and amazing tricks the animals can do. Dolphins are the most popular sea animal shows, but there are also whales doing doing tricks too. If you are lookng for some thrill of your own you can hop on one of the roller coasters or rides to keep you busy and cool with thier water rides.

The above video explains a review on the park. There are roller coasters and rides,but the main attractions are the shows and exhibits of the sea animals.
