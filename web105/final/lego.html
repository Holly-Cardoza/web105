Lego Land

Lego Land is a family park that you dont want to miss. This park is perfect for the littel kids. They have rides for all ages, but with inline lego stations it makes coming to a park for a day a breeze with little ones, as they will not get bored. There are splash stations in the park to help cool off on the hot summer days. You will be amazed by all the scupltors made completly by legos.
